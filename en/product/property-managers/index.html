01.

Sending announcements

With our Equa Delta application, you can send announcements to tenants living in a group of buildings, a building, or all your buildings. Announcements have time information attached to them. An announcement can pertain to an event such as a repair job or a local gathering for example. Tenants can read relevant announcements with the Equa Phi app.

02.

Sending documents

You can send documents to tenants living in a group of buildings, a building, or all your buildings. A document can be a lease document or a house rules document for example.

03.

Tenant groups

You can group tenants by sets of buildings. You can then send your announcements or documents directly to a group for efficient messaging.

Pricing

Affordable pricing with 14-day free trial. Per-company subscription. Company members can purchase an in-app subscription for the company. Subscription plans structured according to the number of managed habitation units.

Test drive

You can test drive our service for free using the Equa Delta app. 1) Create an account in the app. 2) Add your buildings (free), without "publishing" them. 3) Sign into the Equa Phi app with the same account credentials as Equa Delta (Equa Phi is our tenant app). 4) Check into a unit in one of your buildings as a virtual tenant to verify the tenant user experience. 5) To transition into production mode, simply publish your buildings in Equa Delta in order to make our service accessible to your tenants.
