01. Property management

Manage real estate inventory

Add owned or managed buildings to your inventory. Addresses are verified through Google Maps. Attach notes to buildings. Indicate the presence of amenities in buildings, such as shared laundry facilities. Indicating the presence of laundry facilities in your buildings automatically activates the booking function for your residents. For each laundry facility, set the number washing machines and choose a booking calendar from predefined templates.

02. Property management

Manage the stays of residents

Add a stay to a building by specifying the unit identifier, the move-in date, and the name of the resident who signed the rental or sale contract. Add notes to individual stays. Terminate a stay by setting its move-out date. Optionally specify a rent amount for automated rent collection.

03. Property management

Collect rent automatically

Collect rent efficiently by simply entering your bank account number and the rent amounts. Manage rent payments in your dashboard on Stripe.com. Allow household members to split rent, and to change their share of the rent whenever they like. Allow paying residents to change their source bank account getting you involved. Allow paying residents to switch to Equa Phi payments even if they have set up other payment methods. Change the bank account you are using for collecting rent without having to notify your residents. Most Euro-Zone countries are supported using SEPA Direct Debit payments.

05. Property management

Organize your team

Each member of the property management team can specialise in one or more different roles for an efficient division of labor. The person who claims the property management company within Equa World is assigned the "chief" role. Chiefs has full access to all Equa World functions, and they can add other people to the team, be it in a "chief" role, or at least one of "agent", "resident relations manager", "inventory manager", "editor" or "HR manager" roles. Full explanation of each role is provided in the Equa Delta app.

06. Resident experience

Manage your buildings' laundry facilities

Activate the time slot reservation function for your residents by indicating the presence of common laundry facilities in your buildings. For each facility, indicate the number of reservable machines and choose a reservation calendar from predefined templates. If there are several machines, tag them physically starting from "1".

07. Resident experience

Send documents and announcements to residents

With our Equa Delta application, you can send documents and announcements to residents living in a particular unit, a building, a group of buildings, or all your buildings. A document can be a lease document or a house rules document for example. Announcements have an expiration date attached to them, and can pertain to an event such as a repair job or a local gathering for example. Residents can read their documents and announcements with the Equa Phi app.

08. Resident experience

Receive work orders from residents

Receive work orders from your residents. Track progress by setting the work order status, and appending a text note. For more information regarding a request, simply copy the sender's email address and start an email conversation.

09. Resident experience

Efficiently integrate existing residents

We recommend that you use poster-based on-boarding for your existing residents. Just put up posters in your buildings and have your resident scan the QR-code the poster contains. You will need to contact us at support@equa.world so that we can create custom-made posters for each of your buildings, which you would then print out and put up in managed buildings. Note that you can also use this on-boarding method for new residents; in this case they will just have to wait for their move-in date before on-boarding.

10. Resident experience

Integrate new residents

We recommend that you use poster-based on-boarding, but in case there is a reason you wish to on-board a resident before they have moved in, you have two options: (1) Show residents their QR-code on your mobile device using the Equa Delta app; this is convenient if you are meeting the resident in person, for example when signing a contract. (2) Send residents their QR-code in digital or paper form by first saving the QR-code to a file. For both options, you can choose a secret text which you will communicate to the resident to make the on-boarding process more secure.
