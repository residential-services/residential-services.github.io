01.

Receive documents and announcements

The company that manages the building you live in can send you documents, and also announcements that have that have an expiration attached to them. A document can be a lease document or a house rules document for example, and an announcement can pertain to an event such as a repair job or a local gathering.

02.

Book your building's common laundry facilities

Reserve time slots for common laundry facilities. View the list of upcoming reservations.

03.

Send work orders

Send work orders to your property manager. Follow the status of your work order. If your property manager needs more information about your request, he/she may optionally initiate an email conversation with you.

04.

Simple move-in process

To use the Equa Phi app, simply create an account and scan your QR code. Optionally type in the secret code that you were attributed.

How to scan your QR code

You have several options: (1) If your real estate agent or property manager presents you with your QR code on his/her mobile device, then simply scan that screen using Equa Phi. (2) If you have received your QR code in paper form, then scan it using your app. (3) If the QR code is in digital form, then if you have a second mobile device or computer, then scan that second screen. Otherwise, you can photocopy your mobile device screen and then scan the photocopy, or send your QR code to a co-resident and scan his/her screen.
