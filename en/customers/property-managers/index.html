Affordable resident relations management

Simplify the management of resident relations by using the Equa Delta app. Equa Delta complements or replaces your existing communication channels with residents. Typical resident relations management tasks include sending documents and announcements to residents, and receiving work orders. You can read about the features currently available in Equa Delta by heading over to App Store or Play Store.

Offer more comfort to your residents

Make the lives of your residents easier by offering them the Equa Phi app. Most of your residents are used to organizing their lives on the Internet using smartphones, and Equa Phi is a natural fit for them. We plan to include features in Equa Phi that go beyond resident relations, and that are nevertheless useful for residents. Examples: the reservation of shared facilities such as laundry rooms, community features such as pin boards, and easy access to local consumer businesses. You can read about the features currently available in Equa Phi by heading over to App Store or Play Store.

Equa can complement paper

You can continue providing a paper-based user interface to your residents as before if one or more of them asks for it.

Evolving resident feature set

The needs of residents in a large sense is a big part of our focus. Depending on resident needs and requests, we will add new resident-facing features. Some of those will possibly be unrelated to property management, such as a "shopping" feature.

Open-ended

We are attentive to the needs of our property manager customers. New features will be added to Equa over time, depending on customer needs.
